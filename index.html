Welcome to Sol y Mar, where the vibrant spirit of Mexico meets the tranquility of a tropical escape. Tucked away on the shores of the breathtaking Riviera Maya, our resort offers an unforgettable retreat surrounded by azure waters and lush jungle landscapes.

Imagine yourself lounging on powdery white sand beaches, with the gentle rhythm of mariachi music in the air and the aroma of authentic Mexican cuisine wafting from our seaside restaurants. Sip on a refreshing margarita as you bask in the warmth of the sun or savor the flavors of freshly caught seafood prepared by our skilled chefs.

At Sol y Mar, relaxation takes center stage. Indulge in a rejuvenating spa treatment inspired by ancient Mayan rituals, or simply lounge by the infinity pool with a good book and a cool drink in hand. Whether you seek adventure or serenity, Sol y Mar offers the perfect blend of luxury and authenticity for an unforgettable Mexican getaway.
